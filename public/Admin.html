The Authority for Civil Society Organization(ACSO)

The ACSO is a national body established to oversee civil society organizations and their activities. It Create a conducive environment for the full-exercise of freedom of association in accordance with the stipulations of the FDRE Constitution and international agreements ratified by Ethiopia; The organization also Ensure maximum public benefit by supervising organizations carry out their activities in accordance with their registered objectives; It also Build CSO capacity to achieve their set goals and objectives efficiently. And Foster a culture of philanthropy and volunteerism in the society;
